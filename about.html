About Butter Lab

The story, values and people behind our flavored butter.

Our Philosophy

Butter Lab was born from a simple question: what if butter could be as exciting and carefully crafted as coffee, chocolate or wine?

We treat butter as a gourmet product – paying attention to ingredients, textures, visual presentation and the experience of opening each jar.

The project started as a university assignment and quickly became a full concept: brand, digital experience and a catalog of unique flavors. Every decision is based on understanding our customers and their habits at home.

What We Offer

  • Handcrafted flavored butter in small batches.
  • Careful attention to design, packaging and unboxing.
  • Option to build custom gift boxes and hosting kits.
  • Clear information about ingredients and flavor profiles.
  • Simple and trustworthy online ordering experience.
